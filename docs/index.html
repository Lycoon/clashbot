FAQ

Why Clashbot?

I started developing a Discord bot for the clan I was playing in using good-looking pictures as most of Clash of Clans bots at that time were text-based. As I found out it could be useful for any other clasher too, I have extended its possibilities to a larger scale, then Clashbot was born.

Is inviting Clashbot safe?

Clashbot has been officially verified by Discord™. This guarantees the good use of your data privacy. The bot does not communicate with any third-party service in any way.

How is made Clashbot?

The bot is made using Java 16 and few libraries such as a custom Clash of Clans API wrapper.
